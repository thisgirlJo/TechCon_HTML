History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a modest gathering of passionate developers aiming to discuss emerging technological trends and share innovative ideas. Initially centered on software and hardware innovations, the conference rapidly expanded in scope and popularity. By 2015, it had transformed into a global platform, attracting participants worldwide and covering diverse topics such as artificial intelligence (AI), biotechnology, and sustainable technology.

Over the years, TechCon has evolved into one of the largest tech innovation conferences globally, serving as a hub for groundbreaking ideas through its workshops, keynotes, and exhibitions.

Mission

TechCon's mission is to foster innovation and collaboration among technology enthusiasts, professionals, and organizations. The conference aims to create a platform where ideas can flourish, and participants can learn from each other, share knowledge, and inspire future generations of technologists.

Our vision is to be the leading global conference that shapes the future of technology by connecting innovators, thought leaders, and industry experts to drive positive change in society.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted numerous influential speakers from various fields, including:

  • Elon Musk - CEO of SpaceX and Tesla
  • Sundar Pichai - CEO of Alphabet Inc. and Google
  • Satya Nadella - CEO of Microsoft
  • Tim Berners-Lee - Inventor of the World Wide Web
  • Sheryl Sandberg - Former COO of Facebook
